The Donut

Tutorial

First Created: May 2021.

Journal Entry: March 2024.

Following the footsteps of many similar beginners in Blender, my first action was to search up a sufficient tutorial on the legendary resource, Youtube. Thus, I fell upon the Donut Tutorial for Blender 2.8, by the Blender Guru. This tutorial guided me to familiarize myself with the many features of the program, Blender. It consists of 23 unique videos that each go over an essential element of Blender. While this tutorial is far outdated and has since been replaced by a series for Blender 3.0 and later 4.0, it was still very useful to my learning of Blender's capabilities.
